Course Prep Info



Vehicle:
It is highly advisable that you drive a car with 4×4 or AWD when coming to one of our courses. The County Road at times can be very rough and while we usually don’t have mud to get stuck in, there can be a substantial amount of snow or water on the road. Please fill up your car with gas before leaving town. The closest gas stations are located 1 exit south on I-25 and in Clines Corners.

Phones and internet:
Phone and internet reception are limited and spotty in the area. Please be prepared to drive a mile or more to find coverage.

Clothes and Footwear:
All of our courses feature long walks and a substantial amount of time on your feet. The terrain is very rugged and at times can seem daunting. Please wear comfortable, durable and protective footwear.

The School: 
is located partially in a canyon and as such weather conditions can change rapidly. Please be prepared for rain, wind, snow, hail, and hot days no matter the season.

Food and Water:
Please bring plenty of extra food and a large water bottle for yourself. A well is available on the property to refill water bottles. The closest convenience store is located in the village of Villanueva.

Trash and Recycling:
Pack it in pack it out.

Must have gear:
Warm clothes and preferably waterproof clothes, sturdy boots, hat, and extra socks.
Knife (preferably fixed blade), headlamp with extra batteries, notebook and pencil or pen, bic lighter or waterproof matches.
Extra food and plenty of water.
