Intro to Wilderness Survival:
Intro to Wilderness Survival is a one day intensive course that focuses on the most important priorities for a short term survival situation. Intro to Survival will focus on survival mentality and how to maximize your chances of being found as well as how not to get lost or injured in the first place. In a short term survival situation (hunting trip turned dark or sprained ankle on a backcountry hike) you’re best chances of being found are staying put and signaling for help. This course provides an overview of many of the skills, techniques and mindsets that govern our most advanced courses. Intro to Wilderness Survival is a great place for anyone to start regardless of experience. See the course syllabus for more specifics on course content.
